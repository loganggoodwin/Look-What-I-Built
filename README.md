# Look What I Built

**Artist:** Garth Goodwin  
**Project Type:** Original song / music release package  
**Theme:** From nothing to something — a hard-hitting comeback anthem about growth, education, resilience, and proving people wrong.

![Look What I Built Cover](assets/look-what-i-built-cover.png)

## About the Song

**“Look What I Built”** is an in-your-face anthem about rising from a hard beginning and building a better future through discipline, education, and personal grit.

The song tells the story of starting life in the ghetto, being seen as a troublemaker and counted out, then turning that past into fuel. It celebrates earning a **Bachelor’s in Information Technology**, pushing toward a **Master’s in Cybersecurity**, and preparing for the next level: a future **Doctorate in Information Technology**.

This project is about survival, transformation, and ownership of the journey.

## Project Message

They counted me out.  
I counted the wins.

This song is for anyone who started with nothing, made mistakes, took the long road, and still found a way to build something real.
